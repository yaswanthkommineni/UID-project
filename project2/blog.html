How to achieve success in Competitive Programming

The question people ask me frequently is how I became a Master (>2100 rating) in Codeforces. My answer is not going to be practice. Because, I believe practice alone is not sufficient as I came across enough programmers to tell that. The main thing we need in this field is resistance. You need practice for sure, but the resistance plays a key role here. See, this field is highly competitive. Whatever level you may reach, there's always more you can do. It's never ending. You have to choose where to stop. You should put continous effort to improve yourself. In this field, we encounter many obstacles and we need a lot of time and patience to clear those. When you encounter a bug, you should be patient enough to debug. many just give up and move on and that's a very very bad thing to do because most probably you'll encounter the same in the future if you decide to stay in CP and improve yourself. Going beyond your comfort zone is really important. If you try a harder problem, you try to solve it with maximum concentration. There is a breaking point where we give up on the problem. If you don't have resistance, you give up easily and gain nothing. If you have enough resistance and skill, you'll solve the problem. Even if you did not solve that problem, your thinking capability and approaching a solution gets better. It's not at all a waste of time. According to my exeprience, it's definitely better than staying in your comfort zone and solving the problems that you're comfortable with. It's necessary to mention that spending too much time on a single problem may not be a good strategy. Learning things is not a tough part in this field. But, applying right techniques is the key.
